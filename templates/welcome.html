FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

QWIKGAME is an app which facilitates finding partners and arranging 2-player games.

QWIKGAME focuses on 2-player games only with ambitious plans for multi-player games in the future. Currently we have the most popular 2-player games like tennis, squash, table tennis, badminton, racquetball, but also chess, darts, backgammon, billiards and more.

QWIKGAME is free of charge.

QWIKGAME works anywhere in the world.

To find a partner and organize a match start by sending an invitation for a match. Select the game, venue and a timeframe. It is recommended to call the venue and check if there are available playgrounds for your preferred timeframe. Your Match Invitation will be visible in the QWIKFeed to other Players. Multiple Players may respond to your Invitation. You get to choose the best offer and confirm the match. You can also reply to an existing invitation from the QWIKFeed by placing an offer for a particular hour from the given timeframe.

You can post as many invitations as you wish every day and for any game, venue or timeframe.

You can find all current invitations in our QWIKFeed. It is contantly updated with new invitations sent by Players.

The Player who send the initial match invitation is responsible to call and book a court/playground once the match is confirmed. The Player is also recommended to call and check the venue availability before sending the invitation.

QWIKGAME is a Progressive Web App. It's the new generation of apps. No need to download nor install anything. Just save QWIKGAME on your Home screen. QWIKGAME PWA works on all operating systems and an Internet Browser is the only requirement.
